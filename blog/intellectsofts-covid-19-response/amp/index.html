Intellectsoft’s COVID-19 Response

Anastasiia Hibaieva
1 week ago

Dear Intellectsoft Community,

As the COVID-19 situation continues to evolve, so does our response. We want to keep everyone informed about how we are acting as a company.

First and foremost, the health and safety of our community has been — and will always be — our top priority. Social distancing is what we all need to be doing right now. As of last week, all Intellectsoft employees, with just a few exceptions, have been working from home with a secure and seamless connection. We have also canceled all company travels and events.

We’ve put business continuity plans in place to ensure that our core systems and services continue to operate as usual during this period. We are fully committed to continue providing our customers and partners with the products and services they depend on.

Our senior leadership team remains vigilant and is monitoring the situation in real-time and responding rapidly as conditions change. If you have any questions, our support teams continue to be available and will be able to respond within our usual timeframes.

At Intellectsoft, we are committed to doing all it takes to protect and support our community. We will coordinate our responses, take strong and collective action, and get through this together.

Be safe and stay healthy.

 

Vladimirs Vahromovs,

CEO and Board Member at Intellectsoft LLC
